About
Alfabiz Informatics

Alfabiz informatics is a software solution provider company based in Nairobi Kenya.

Started in 2010, AlfaBiz Informatics limited is a fully owned and operated Kenyan Consultancy specialising in ERP solutions, Payroll/HRM, Time & Attendance, Bespoke Applications, Business Intelligence and Software Automation and Integration for Small to Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs).

Alfabiz Informatics has grown to be one of the leading accounting software providers that helped hundreds of growing businesses in Kenya to improve their process efficiency, streamline systems and increase the value of their business.

Information Systems for Small to Medium-sized Enterprises

Whether you are looking for Accounting solutions, Payroll, Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Time & Attendance, Inventory Management, Job & Project Costing, Point of Sale (POS), Fixed Assets, Analytics and Reporting or a range of other systems, we take time to understand your exact needs through a needs analysis of your daily business activities and present you with the best solution for your business, together with any advice and support you may need.

As your business grows, so does its complexity and staying organized can be a real challenge. Many small companies start out their accounting system using multiple excel spreadsheets and a basic off-the-shelf software package. These solutions are often a pretty good way to start with; however, as your business grows your current accounting software solution may no longer be suitable for your business.

Our solutions are fully integrated ERP systems which will give you a true business management view of your entire organization and operation, our ERP systems provide you with a clear understanding of what’s driving your business, with the ability to drill down to the details behind every transaction.

If you think it may be time to consider a new system, we can help you customize your new software to match the unique needs of your business and specific industry requirements.
